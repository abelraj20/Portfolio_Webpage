About Me

My Projects

Europe fuel prices project was an interactive visualisation for the petrol and diesel prices in
EU countries from 2005-2023. Included a dynamic map with features like scrolling, zooming,
and clicking. Upon selecting a country, the tool displayed a graph of average fuel prices over
time, offering insights into regional price patterns.

Developed a Connect 4 game in Python. In this terminal-based game, two players competed
to align four pieces on a 7x6 grid either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Kolmogorov Flow visualisation project which explored fluid instability in two-dimensional incompressible flows through complex mathematical models. Langrangian Particle Tracking (LPT), Reynolds numbers and vorticity were studied.
